How it works
  • Each PDF transaction is matched to a Money Manager entry within ±3 days at the same amount.
  • If the amount is off by ≤5% within ±5 days we flag it as inaccurate for review.
  • Anything else flags as missing — fix it on your phone, then re-upload to re-analyse.
  • MM entries that no PDF row consumed are listed as unused.
  • Nothing is written to Google Sheets until you click Confirm & Push.
